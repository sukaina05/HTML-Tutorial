My Top 5 Favourite Movies

  1. 3 idiots
    • Year:2009
    • Main Actors: Aamir khan,R.Madhavan,Sharman Joshi,and Kareena Kapoor
    • Brief Description: It's a comedy-drama film based on the story of engineering college students.The film explores themes of friendship ,education,and the true purpose of life.It aims to make the audience laugh,think,and be inspired.The story ,direction,and performances in this film were highly appreciated,and it became a huge hit.
  2. Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara
    • Year:2011
    • Main Actors: Hrithik Roshan,Farhan Akhtar,Abhay Deol,Katrina Kaif, and Kalki Koechlin.
    • Brief Description: The film follows the story of three friends who embark on a road trip in spain before one of them gets married.It's a beautiful tale of friendship, self-discovery, and living life to the fullest.The film showcases breathtaking locations,thrilling adventures, and heartfelt moments."zindagi na milegi dobara" received critical acclaim for its performances,direction, and screenplay.It's a must-watch if you haven't seen it yet!
  3. Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani
    • Year:2013
    • Main Actors:Ranbir Kapoor,Deepika Padukone,Aditya Roy Kapoor,Kalki Koechlin,Kunaal Roy Kapur,Farooq shaikh,Vinita Amar
    • Brief Description: "yeh jawani hai deewani" is a bollywood film directed by ayan mukerji.It's a coming-of-age romantic drama that follows the journey of four friends,Bunny,Naina,Aditi,and Avi, as they reunite after several years for a wedding.The movie explores themes of love,friendship,dreams,and self-discovery. it's a fun and emotional rollercoaster that captures the essence of youth and the pursuit of dreams.
  4. My name is Khan
    • Year:2010
    • Main Actors:Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol
    • Brief Description: A bollywood film directed by karan johar ,the movie tells the story of rizwan khan,played by shah rukh khan, a muslim man asperger syndrome.After facing discrimination and tragedy in his life,rizwan embarks on a journey to meet the president of the united states and convey a message of peace and acceptance.The film explores themes of love and the power of unity in the face of adversity. "My name is khan "is a touching and impactful movie.
  5. Agneepath
    • Year: 2012
    • Main Actors:Hrithik Roashan,Sanjay Dutt,and Priyanka Chopra
    • Brief Description: "Agneepath" is an action-packed bollywood film that tells the story of vijay deenanath chauhan,played by hrithik roshan.He seeks revenge against the ruthless kancha cheena,portrayed by sanjay dutt,who murdered his father. The performances and intense action sequences make it a must-watch for fans of action-packed dramas.
